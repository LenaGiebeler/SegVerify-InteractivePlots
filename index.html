In search of truth: Evaluating concordance of AI-based anatomy segmentation models

This website presents the interactive plots accompanying the report “In search of truth: Evaluating concordance of AI-based anatomy segmentation models.” The startpage shows Volume and Dice plots for the groups Lung, Heart, Ribs, Vertebrae, and Sternum, each including all models considered in the analysis. For some groups, dedicated sub-pages provide additional plots based on our findings. All plots are fully interactive: you can zoom, pan, and toggle models in the legend. Use Open to launch the respective plot in a new window.
