In search of truth: Evaluating concordance of AI-based anatomy segmentation models

This website presents the interactive plots accompanying the report “In search of truth: Evaluating concordance of AI-based anatomy segmentation models.” The startpage shows interactive Volume and Dice plots for all structure groups included in the analysis, with each plot including all models that provide a segmentation for the respective structure. For some groups, dedicated sub-pages provide additional plots based on our findings. All plots are fully interactive: you can zoom, pan, and toggle models in the legend. Use Open to launch the respective plot in a new window.
